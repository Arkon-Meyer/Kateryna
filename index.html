How I Work

Every painting is a journey — from the first pencil sketch to the final brushstroke in Corel Painter. Each piece takes 40 to 60 hours of focused, hand-crafted work.

From Concept to Canvas

01

Pencil Sketch

Every painting begins with traditional pencil sketches — exploring composition, poses, and the emotional tone of the piece.

02

Values & Composition

A monochrome value study establishes light, shadow, and depth — the foundation that makes the final painting read at any size.

03

Color & Atmosphere

Color is layered in gradually, building mood and atmosphere. Each hue is chosen to serve the story the painting tells.

04

Final Painting

Hours of detailed brushwork in Corel Painter bring the piece to life — every texture, reflection, and strand of hair painted by hand.

About

Katarina Sokolova

Digital illustrator & Corel Painter Master

I'm a CG artist who has worked as a freelance illustrator since 2000. My style blends realism, fantasy, sensuality, and storytelling — placing characters and objects in surreal worlds of dreams and imagination.

I create digital illustrations for international editorials, advertising agencies, publishing houses, and magazines across five continents. My work has been featured on book covers, puzzles, calendars, and in the world's leading digital art publications.

In 2016, I was honoured with the Corel Painter Master Award. My artwork was selected for the cover and advertising campaign of Corel Painter XI, and I've served as an official beta tester for the software since 2007.
